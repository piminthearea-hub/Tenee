# Tenee O-1A Evidence Binder — Capture Plan

## 1. Binder Purpose

This binder documents the impact, reach, and recognition of Tenee — a free, privacy-first public tool that helps elders, caregivers, and expats prepare Thailand immigration paperwork. The evidence collected here supports an O-1A extraordinary ability petition by demonstrating original contribution, sustained national/international recognition, and quantifiable real-world impact of the tool and its creator.

## 5. Compliance Rules for Evidence

1. **Always blur personal data** in all screenshots (names, faces, passport numbers).
2. **Do not store or circulate** passport images, visa stamps, or bank statements.
3. **Keep only aggregate metrics** — never individual user data.
4. **Maintain consistency**: the statement "We do not sell your data" must appear in all public-facing copy and in binder documentation.
5. **Date-stamp everything**: every piece of evidence should include a capture date.
6. **No founder names in PDF footers** — only "Generated by Tenee (free) — tenee.app".
