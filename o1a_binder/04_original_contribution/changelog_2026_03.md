# Changelog — March 2026

## 2026-03-03 — v1.0 Launch

### Added
- **Preparation Packet Generator**: Wizard-based flow for Retirement, Visa Extension, 90-Day Reporting, TM30, Re-entry Permit.
- **Write in Thai**: Polite Thai letter builder for landlords, banks, juristic offices, insurance, immigration, and government agencies.
- **Safety & Allergy**: Tabbed safety page with Allergy Card generator (Thai/English), Taxi Card templates, Medical Help, Lost & Directions, Basic Thai Phrases.
- **Privacy Promise**: "We do not sell your data" added to landing page, footer, about page, feedback page, and PDF cover.
- **Elder-friendly design**: Large fonts (18–20px base), high-contrast UI, buttons ≥ 56px.
- **Caregiver Mode**: Create packets on behalf of elders.
- **Print Mode**: One-click print-friendly formatting.
- **Fun Facts & Thailand Card**: Daily rotating Thai dish/slang card and province fun facts.
- **Scam Warning**: Persistent safety banner.
- **Common Mistakes**: Quick-tip micro-panel.

### Privacy & Compliance
- Passport field restricted to last 4 digits only.
- Feedback page requires no-sensitive-data checkbox.
- PDF footer: "Generated by Tenee (free) — tenee.app" (no founder names, no QR).
- TM30 wording: "proof/checklist" — never "you must file TM30."
